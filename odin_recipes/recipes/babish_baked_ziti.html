Roasted Red Pepper Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 lb rigatoni
  • 8 ounces low moisture, mozzarrella cheese, chopped into chunks
  • 16 oz cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 oz grated parm
  • Nutmeg
  • Sausage/Beef meat sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
Steps
  1. Boild 1 pound of rigatoni
  2. Mix cottage cheese, eggs, 2 oz grated parm and nutmeg
  3. Add chopped basil
  4. Pour mixture, meat sauce, and bechamel into the pasts.
  5. Add 8 ounce chunks of mozzarrella
  6. Pour into pan, cover with 1/2 pound shredded mozz and grated parm
  7. Bake at 350F for 30 min, covered. Uncover and bake for 20 more min.
