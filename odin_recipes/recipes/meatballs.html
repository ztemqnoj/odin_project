MEATBALLS

Ingredients

  • 1 up bread crumbs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 egg
  • Seasoning - Salt, pepper, crushed red pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, oregano
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 pound sweet italian sauage
  • Grated parmesan
  • Sauce of your choice
Steps
  1. Soak bread crumbs in milk
  2. Combine all ingredients. Season to taste
  3. Roll meat mixture into balls and place on baking sheet
  4. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes
  5. Put in sauce to finish
