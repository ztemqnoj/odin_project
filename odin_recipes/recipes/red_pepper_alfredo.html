Roasted Red Pepper Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian Sauasge (Sweet or Hot)
  • 4 chopped cloves of garlic
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive oil
  • 1 and 1/2 cups milk
  • 32 Oz Chicken Broth
  • 1 (16 oz) jar Roasted Red Peppers, drained, pureed
  • 4-8 oz Goat Cheese
  • Grated parmesan, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound of Cavatappi Pasta
Steps
  1. Brown sausage with garlic and olive oil
  2. Stir in chicken broth, pepper puree, milk, and pasta. Add salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer until pasta is cooked
  4. When pasta is done, add goat cheese and grated parmesan.
  5. Serve!
